Education

I am a junior at Case Western Reserve University, and I am a dual degree student triple majoring in Systems and Control Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Music.

Systems and Control Engineering

I was introduced to systems engineering through the systems engineering workship at the Naval Academy Summer Seminar.

I am drawn to the nature of systems engineering in that it brings together a variety of engineering disciplines into one design.

As a systems engineer, I am interested in signal processing and designing automated systems.

Computer Engineering

My pursuit of computer engineering is representative of my interests in digital circuits, coding, and everything in between.

My education for this major exposes me to everything from coding and algorithm design, to circuits and hardware synthesis.

As a computer engineer, I enjoy logic synthesis and mathematics as they apply to signal processing.

Music

I have been at the piano since I was six years old.

In the spring of 2015, I was selected as a winner of CWRU's annual concerto competition. As a result, I performed Prokofiev's Piano Concerto No. 1 at the Milton and Tamar Maltz Performing Arts Center with the Case/University Circle Symphony Orchestra.

I currently pay for my education through a full tuition music scholarship.
